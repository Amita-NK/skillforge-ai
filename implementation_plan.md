This "National Finalist" plan transforms SkillForge into a high-impact digital ecosystem designed for every learner in India, from urban tech hubs to the remotest rural villages.

### [Social Impact Engine: Built for Bharat]
A centralized hub (`ImpactHub`) to toggle specialized learning modes:

1.  **Rural-First Mode**: Ultra-lite UI, offline packs (S3), 2G-optimized sync.
2.  **Regional Language Bridge**: Bedrock-powered multilingual tutoring (Tamil, Malayalam, Hindi, etc.).
3.  **ForgeSense Accessibility**: Voice control and screen-reader optimizations for differently-abled students.
4.  **Mental Wellness (CFI)**: AI fatigue monitoring and "Burnout Guardian" notifications.
5.  **Empowerment Tracks**: Specially curated paths for Women Re-entry and First-Generation Learners.
6.  **Government School Kit**: AI Teaching Assistant dashboard for classroom management.

### [Rewards & Livelihood]
- **Skill-to-Earn Vault**: 3D view for scholarships, physical rewards (Tech Kits), and local gig matching.
- **Employability Builder**: AI resume generator and interview simulator.

### [3D Immersive UI]
- **Aura 2.0 (Holographic Assistant)**: Persistent 3D entity available for "Live Help" via the [HelpAssistant](file:///c:/Users/Amita%20Narayanan/OneDrive/Desktop/SkillForgeAI/web/src/components/layout/HelpAssistant.tsx#10-104).
